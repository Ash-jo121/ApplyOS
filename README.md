# ApplyOS

> A personal job application operating system that monitors target companies, evaluates job relevance, and prepares ATS-safe, JD-aligned resume variants with human-in-the-loop controls.

---

## 🎯 Why ApplyOS Exists

Applying to roles repeatedly requires:

- Manually tracking company career pages
- Rewriting resumes for every job description
- Ensuring ATS compatibility
- Avoiding keyword stuffing or hallucinated claims

ApplyOS was built to reduce **application fatigue** while preserving **intent, correctness, and control**.

It assists — it does not auto-apply.

---

## 🧠 What ApplyOS Does

1. Monitors configured company career pages
2. Detects roles matching target job titles
3. Extracts and normalizes job descriptions
4. Evaluates resume ↔ JD relevance
5. Generates ATS-safe resume mutations using rule-constrained AI
6. Produces structured diffs before final approval

---

## 🚫 What ApplyOS Explicitly Does NOT Do

- ❌ Does not auto-apply to jobs
- ❌ Does not bypass official job portals
- ❌ Does not invent experience or skills
- ❌ Does not mass-generate identical resumes
- ❌ Does not scrape aggressively or violate platform terms

ApplyOS is designed as a **human-in-the-loop system**, not a bot.
